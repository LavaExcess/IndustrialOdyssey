# Industrial Odyssey

**Industrial Odyssey** is a large-scale technical modpack that will take you through all stages of the industrial revolution: from smoking steam-powered machines to distant planets. A true technological journey filled with discoveries, automation, and epic battles awaits you.

---

## 🚀 Main Milestones of Your Journey

- **From Steam to Electricity** - start with primitive smelters and steam mechanisms from **Industrial Upgrade**, gradually mastering electricity and advanced alloys.
- **The Thermal Era** - harness the power of **Thermal Series** machines: process ores, generate energy, and build complex production chains.
- **Forestry and Genetics** - breed bees, cultivate trees, and create new species with **Forestry** and **Gendustry**.
- **Conquering Space** - with **Ad Astra**, build a rocket, travel to the Moon and Mars, mine rare resources, and establish bases on other planets.
- **The Digital Age** - automate everything with **Applied Energistics 2**, its addons (**MegaCells**, **AE2 Things**), and programmable computers from **CC:Tweaked**.

---

## 🌍 A World Full of Life and Danger

- **Biomes** - dozens of new dimensions and biomes from **Oh The Biomes We've Gone**, **Biomes O' Plenty**, **Nullscape**, and others.
- **Mobs** - fight dragons from **Ice and Fire**, encounter spirits from **Born in Chaos**, explore the ocean depths with **Alex's Caves** and **Aquamirae**.
- **Structures** - discover ruins, abandoned mines, and massive dungeons filled with treasures and dangers.

---

## 🎯 Progression Through Quests

Forget about feeling lost - detailed **FTB Quests** will guide you through every stage of development: from your first ore ingot to an interplanetary traveler. Every step is rewarded, and complex recipes have been reworked for smooth and logical progression.
